## Notable Outliers

- **Georgia**: No ethics CPE mandate (only state besides SD)
- **South Dakota**: No ongoing ethics CPE (initial licensure only); 100% self-study allowed
- **Minnesota**: 8hr ethics (double the norm); 24hr group learning minimum
- **Alabama**: 8hr A&A required for ALL CPAs (not just attest) — highest in nation
- **New Mexico**: 80% technical floor (96 of 120 hours must be technical)
- **Indiana**: 50% self-study cap (most states have no cap)
- **Wisconsin**: 40hr formal/instructor-led minimum
