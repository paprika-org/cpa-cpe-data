## Key Patterns

**Annual deadline states** (40hr/yr): Alabama, Mississippi, Nevada, New York, North Carolina, South Carolina
**Birth-month states** (rolling 3yr): California, New Mexico, Oklahoma, Texas
**Dec 31 biennial states** (80hr/2yr): Colorado, District of Columbia, Louisiana, Maryland, Tennessee, Utah, Virginia
**Jun 30 states** (common for fiscal-year states): Arizona, Connecticut, Florida, Idaho, Kansas, Kentucky, Massachusetts, Michigan, Oregon, Vermont
