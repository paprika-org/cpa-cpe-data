## 2026 Dec 31 Deadline Checklist

- [ ] Confirm your state is in the 2026 active cycle
- [ ] Count your completed CPE hours (including carryover if allowed)
- [ ] Confirm ethics requirement met with correct provider
- [ ] Check A&A requirement if you perform attest services
- [ ] Verify CPE sponsors are NASBA QAS approved (most states require this)
- [ ] Keep certificates for 5 years minimum
- [ ] Submit renewal by December 31 (not just CPE — the renewal itself)
